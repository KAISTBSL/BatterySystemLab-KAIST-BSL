Welcome to KAIST Battery System LAB (BSL)!

Prof. Yoonkoo Lee and our Researchers conduct research on batteries for future mobility.

Our work spans from material-level analyses for battery safety to the investigation of internal degradation mechanisms and thermal runaway phenomena, which are then applied to system-level modeling and optimization.

Welcome

We are recruiting students and researchers at various positions (Master’s, Ph.D., Postdoctoral researchers, and interns) who are interested in secondary battery research related to mobility systems. The specific research areas include:

  • Analysis and prediction of secondary batteries
  • Battery system-integrated design and optimization
  • System-level control and demonstration
